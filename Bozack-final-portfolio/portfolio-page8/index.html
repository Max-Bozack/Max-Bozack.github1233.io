Plot

The protagonist, Itadori Yuji, has found himself joining forces with Jujutsu sorcers after becoming possed by an ancient curse known as Ryuomen Sukuna. Yuji is given a choice: be executed because of the danger he poses to the world, or become a Jujutsu sorcer and collect the remaining pieces of Sukuna's power. He chooses to live, and learns to harness his new found powers with the help of his teammates and mentor.

Jujutsu Kaisen 0

Jujutsu Kaisen 0 is a movie coming out on December 24th 2021, and is a prequel to Season 1. The story takes place 1 year prior to the events in Season 1. Yuji, Megumi and Nobara will likely not appear in this movie. However, there will be several other returning characters including Gojo, Panda, Maki, Toge and Geto as seen on the cover. The main focus of the movie will be on a character named Yuta Okkotsu.
