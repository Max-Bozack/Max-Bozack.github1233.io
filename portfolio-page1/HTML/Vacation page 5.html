Bali

Information about Bali

Bali is an island in the country of Indonesia. Bali is known for its forested volcanic mountains, beaches, coral reefs, and local food. Religious sites such as the Uluwatu Temple are found on Bali. There are also several popular resort towns that are known for being great yoga and meditation retreats.

Why I reccomend Bali

Bali is home to so many different beautiful destinations. There are coral reefs and beaches, which are fantastic for avid swimmers like myself. If oyu want to visit an old mountain side temple, with beautiful scenery all around, Bali is the place for you. You can get immersed in teh culture of the island or have a relaxing vacation to yourself at one of the many famous meditation retreats. All of this can be found on the island of Bali, which is home to a little more than four million people.
